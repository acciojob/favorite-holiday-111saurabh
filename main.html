Maldives


The Maldives is a stunning tropical paradise known for its crystal-clear waters, white sandy beaches, luxurious resorts, and vibrant marine life. It’s the perfect escape for relaxation, adventure, and natural beauty.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Explore the beautiful coral reefs through snorkeling. Coral reefs
  2. Relax at the pristine beaches on private islands. Pristine beaches
  3. Experience underwater dining at exclusive restaurants.
  4. Take a scenic sunset cruise with dolphin watching.
  5. Stay in a luxurious overwater villa with amazing views.

Must-Try Foods

  • Mas Huni — a traditional Maldivian breakfast dish.
  • Garudhiya — a flavorful fish soup served with rice.
  • Fihunu Mas — delicious Maldivian grilled fish.
  • Hedhikaa — assorted Maldivian short eats (snacks).
  • Saagu Bondibai — a sweet sago pudding.
